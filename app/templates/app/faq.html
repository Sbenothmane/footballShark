How it Works

Spread: Since the teams are usually not evenly matched, we set the spread to make betting fair. The spread is applied to the home teams score at the end of the game. A negative spread means the home team is favored. -3 is generally the starting point with home field advantage taken into account.

Over/Under: This allows you to wager on how many total points are scored in the game. You can expect 90% of all games to fall between 40-50.


Before the Games

Betting: You will have a chance to bet three units on both the Spread and the Over/Under for each game this week. (The example below shows only three)


Game 1: Bills @ Vikings, spread:0 over/under:45. A spread of zero means the teams are evenly matched, with home field taken into account. This is also called pickum, and the team that wins on the field also wins against the spread. You can see that I bet two units on the Bills and two units on the Over. Meaning, I think the Bills will win, and that the total number of points scored will be above 45.

Game 2: Colts @ Eagles, spread:-7.5 over/under:49.5. The spread is -7.5, which means the Eagles must win by more than a touchdown to cover. I bet 1 on the Colts and 1 on the Under. This means I think the Eagles may win, but not by more than 7.5 - and that the total number of points scores will be below 49.5.

Game 3: Broncos @ Ravens, spread:-3 over/under:41. The spread is -3, which means the teams would be even on a neutral site. I bet three on the Ravens, and none on the over/under (You can bet 0 for as many games (or weeks) as you wish). This means I think the Ravens will win by at least three points, and that I have no opinion on the total.

After the Games


Results:

Bills 7 Ravens 0

Colts 30 Eagles 37

Broncos 0 Ravens 17



Game 1: Bills won by more than 0, and the total was Under because 7 + 0 < 45

Game 2: Eagles failed to win by more than 7.5, and therefore lost {Eagles score (37) - 7.5 = 29.5. Colts 30 > Eagles 29.5}. The total was Over because 30 + 37 > 49.5

Game 3: Ravens won by more than 3, and the total was Under because 0 + 17 < 41


As you can see below, my bets on Bills, Colts, and Ravens were all winners, while my bets on the Bills game Over and Colts game Under were not so good.
